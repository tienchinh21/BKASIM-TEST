# Product Overview

BioQMiniapp is a Zalo Mini App for business networking and community management. The application serves as a platform for professional groups (referred to as "Giba") to manage memberships, events, meetings, and networking activities.

## Core Features

- **Group Management**: Create and manage professional groups with membership approval workflows
- **Event System**: Event registration, guest invitations, check-in functionality, and event history tracking
- **Member Directory**: Browse members, view profiles, and manage member details with role-based access
- **Networking Tools**: REF (referral) system, appointments, showcases, and achievements tracking
- **Meeting Scheduling**: Create and manage meetings with attendance tracking
- **News & Updates**: Article management and news feed for community updates
- **Profile Management**: Public profiles with shareable links, membership status badges

## User Roles

The app supports different user roles with varying permissions:

- Regular members
- Group administrators
- System administrators

## Approval Workflow

New users go through an approval process with three states:

- Pending approval (status: 0)
- Approved (status: 1)
- Rejected (status: 2)

Users with pending or rejected status have limited access to features.
